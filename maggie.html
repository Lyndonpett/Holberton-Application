Meet Maggie

Meet Maggie, she is Green Cheek Conure as well. You may be asking: why does she look so different than Larry? She looks different because she is a different color mutation than Larry. Larry is a natural mutation while Maggie is a cinnamon mutation. Maggie's has a much bigger attitude than her brother. She will bite when she does not get what she wants. We have had her for a little over two years now.
