Meet Larry

Meet Larry, he is a Green Cheeked Conure. I have had him as a pet for over four years now and he is the reason I got more parrots. Larry cannot talk, but he does try to be vocal and will make sounds along the lines of "Meep Mop". He absolutely loves my girlfriend and chose her over me when we moved in together.
