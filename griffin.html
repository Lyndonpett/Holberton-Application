Meet Griffin

Meet Griffin, he is different from the other two and that is because he is a cockatiel. Cockatiels typically are more mild mannered than conures. Conures like to run around, climb into things, and cause all kinds of chaos. Griffin likes to find a nice place to perch and will sit there all day. Griffin is my little companion and will not let my girlfriend handle him. If I leave the room, he will be flying right behind me to make sure I am always in sight. Griffin likes to sing his flight call. His flight call is a little whistle he does and if you mimic it with your hand out, he will proceed to fly to your hand.
